# Macroeconomics

## ECO 114: Macroeconomic Principles (3 credits)

Teaches basic macroeconomic principles including national income accounting, business cycles, income determination, monetary and fiscal problems and policy. Also covers international economics including trade, comparative advantage, balance of payments, exchange rates, and trade and finance problems and policy. No prerequisites.

## ECO 314: Intermediate Macroeconomics (3 credits)

Develops analytical capability regarding aggregate demand and supply, employment, inflation, national output, international trade, and macroeconomic policies. Prerequisite: ECO 114.

## ECO 414: Applied Macroeconomics: Case Studies (3 credits)

Covers advanced macroeconomic issues including long-term growth, short-term fluctuations, and policy analysis through case studies and journal articles. Prerequisites: ECO 113, ECO 114, ECO 314, junior standing.
