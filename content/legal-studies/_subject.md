# Legal Studies

The study of law as it applies to business and society. Bryant's legal studies program covers contract law, torts, business organizations, employment law, and intellectual property.

## Course Areas
- **Business Law** (LCS 201) -- Contracts, torts, business organizations
- **Commercial Law** -- UCC, sales, secured transactions
- **Employment Law** -- Workplace regulation, discrimination, labor relations
- **International Law** -- Trade law, dispute resolution
