# Communication

The study of how messages are created, transmitted, and interpreted. Bryant's communication program covers public speaking, media, interpersonal communication, and professional writing.

## Course Areas
- **Public Speaking** -- Rhetoric, persuasion, presentation skills
- **Media Studies** -- Mass communication, digital media, journalism
- **Business Communication** -- Professional writing, presentations, reports
- **Interpersonal Communication** -- Listening, conflict, relationship dynamics
- **Organizational Communication** -- Internal comms, culture, leadership communication
