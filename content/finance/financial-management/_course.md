# Financial Management (FIN 201)

The foundational finance course at Bryant University. Covers the financial management of the business enterprise and the role of the financial manager in value creation. Major topics include time value of money, risk and return, security valuation, capital budgeting, cash and liquidity management, working capital, dividend policy, cost of capital, and capital structure.

## Prerequisites
- BUS 100
- Co-requisite: MATH 201 or AM 231

## Chapter Sequence
1. Time Value of Money (Present and Future Value)
2. Annuities and Complex Cash Flows
3. Financial Statements and Ratio Analysis
4. Risk and Return
5. Bond Valuation
6. Stock Valuation
7. Capital Budgeting (NPV, IRR, Payback)
8. Cost of Capital
9. Capital Structure and Leverage
10. Working Capital Management
11. Derivatives and Options Basics
