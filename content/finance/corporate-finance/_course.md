# Corporate Finance (FIN 310 / FIN 460)

FIN 310 provides in-depth exploration of corporate financial management: capital budgeting, capital structure, dividend policy, working capital management, and valuation techniques with Excel data analysis. FIN 460 is the capstone covering corporate valuation, real options, M&A, LBOs, and managerial compensation through case studies.

## Prerequisites
- FIN 310: FIN 201, Sophomore Standing
- FIN 460: FIN 201/201G, FIN 310, FIN 312, Junior Standing
