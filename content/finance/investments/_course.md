# Investments (FIN 312)

Broad perspective on investment objectives and determinants of investment decision making. Students are introduced to characteristics of investment vehicles, market function/operation, measurement of returns and risks, and analytical pricing techniques. Portfolio management is the framework for security-pricing models. Held in the Financial Market Center (FMC) with real-time data.

## Prerequisites
- FIN 201 and MATH 201
