# Data Science and Analytics

Extracting insights from data to drive business decisions. Bryant's analytics program covers statistical modeling, visualization, machine learning, and business intelligence.

## Course Areas
- **Intro to Data Analytics** -- Descriptive, predictive, prescriptive analytics
- **Data Visualization** -- Charts, dashboards, storytelling with data
- **Predictive Analytics** -- Regression, classification, forecasting
- **Business Intelligence** -- Data warehousing, ETL, reporting tools
- **Big Data** -- Distributed computing, unstructured data, NoSQL
