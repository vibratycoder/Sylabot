# Sociology

The systematic study of human society, social institutions, and social relationships. Bryant's sociology courses examine inequality, culture, deviance, and social change.

## Course Areas
- **Intro to Sociology** (SOC 150) -- Sociological perspectives, culture, stratification
- **Social Problems** -- Inequality, poverty, crime, health disparities
- **Race and Ethnicity** -- Prejudice, discrimination, systemic racism
- **Gender Studies** -- Gender roles, feminism, workplace equality
