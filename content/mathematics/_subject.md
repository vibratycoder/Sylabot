# Mathematics

Foundational quantitative skills for business and science. Bryant's math offerings emphasize business applications including calculus, linear algebra, and discrete math.

## Course Areas
- **Precalculus** -- Functions, algebra, trigonometry
- **Business Calculus** (MATH 201) -- Derivatives, integrals, optimization
- **Calculus I/II** -- Limits, differentiation, integration, series
- **Linear Algebra** -- Matrices, vectors, systems of equations
- **Discrete Mathematics** -- Logic, sets, combinatorics, graph theory
