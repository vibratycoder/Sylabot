# Principles of Managerial Accounting (ACG 204)

Explores how accounting information is used internally by management to determine product/service cost, understand cost behavior, plan/evaluate/control operations, and make business decisions.

## Prerequisites
- ACG 203 (Principles of Financial Accounting)

## Chapter Sequence
1. Cost-Volume-Profit (CVP) Analysis
2. Budgeting and Variance Analysis
3. Cost Accounting Fundamentals (Job-Order and Process Costing)
