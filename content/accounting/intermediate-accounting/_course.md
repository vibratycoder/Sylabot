# Financial Reporting I & II (ACG 301 / ACG 302)

Advanced financial reporting topics for creditors, investors, regulatory agencies, and other parties. ACG 301 covers conceptual development and reporting alternatives. ACG 302 covers pensions, leases, long-term debt, and stockholders' equity.

## Prerequisites
- Grade of "C" or higher in ACG 203 and junior standing
- ACG 302 requires grade of "C" or higher in ACG 301

## Key Topics Covered
- ACG 301: Revenue recognition depth, receivables, inventory valuation, fixed assets, intangibles
- ACG 302: Pensions, leases (ASC 842), long-term debt, stockholders' equity, EPS, deferred taxes
- ACG 461: Business combinations and consolidations
