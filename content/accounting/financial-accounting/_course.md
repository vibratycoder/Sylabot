# Principles of Financial Accounting (ACG 203)

The introductory financial accounting course at Bryant University. Students understand how fundamental Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) drive the creation of financial information. Common uses of financial information for performance evaluation by internal and external decision-makers are explored.

## Prerequisites
- BUS 100 (pre/co-requisite)

## Chapter Sequence
1. Accounting Fundamentals (Debits, Credits, The Accounting Equation)
2. Journal Entries and T-Accounts
3. The Income Statement
4. The Balance Sheet
5. The Statement of Cash Flows
6. GAAP Principles and the Conceptual Framework
7. Adjusting Entries
8. Closing Entries and the Accounting Cycle
9. Inventory Methods
10. Depreciation Methods
