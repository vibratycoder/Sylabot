# Accounting

The language of business -- measuring, processing, and communicating financial information. Bryant's accounting program covers financial reporting, managerial decision-making, taxation, auditing, and accounting information systems.

## Bryant University Course Catalog (2025-2026)

### Core / Introductory
- **ACG 101** Accounting Careers -- Introduction to business and accounting topics for pre-college students
- **ACG 203** Principles of Financial Accounting -- GAAP fundamentals, financial statement creation, performance evaluation
- **ACG 204** Principles of Managerial Accounting -- Product/service costing, cost behavior, planning, controlling, business decisions

### Intermediate Financial Reporting
- **ACG 301** Financial Reporting I -- Financial reporting for creditors/investors/regulators, reporting alternatives
- **ACG 302** Financial Reporting II -- Pensions, leases, long-term debt, stockholders' equity
- **ACG 461** Financial Reporting III -- Business combinations and consolidations

### Cost / Managerial
- **ACG 311** Cost Management -- Product costing, planning/controlling, continuous improvement, decision-making
- **ACG 315** Advanced Managerial Accounting -- Balanced scorecard, strategy maps, target costing, lean manufacturing, quality costs, lifecycle costs
- **ACG 471** Product/Service Costing -- Designing/measuring costs in different operating environments

### Tax
- **ACG 351** Corporate Taxation -- Corporation taxation, interpretation of tax law, shareholder taxation
- **ACG 352** Individual Taxation -- Federal tax structure, income determination, exemptions, deductions, property transactions, tax planning

### Systems and Technology
- **ACG 345** Accounting Information Systems -- AIS theory/practice, database systems, decision support, controls, systems analysis
- **ACG 381** Information Systems Controls and Audit -- IT auditing, digital evidence, IT risk/controls, ISACA certification
- **ACG 445** Data Analytics in Accounting -- Big data in accounting, advanced analytics, business performance
- **ACG ST300** Robotic Process Automation in Accounting -- RPA tools, automation of accounting/audit/tax tasks

### Auditing
- **ACG 442** Auditing Concepts and Applications -- Audit of financial statements, professional responsibilities, risk analysis, audit evidence, data analytics

### Specialized
- **ACG 320** International Accounting -- Financial reporting across geographic boundaries, IFRS vs GAAP
- **ACG 350** Fraud Examination -- Causes/consequences of fraud, fraud examination procedures
- **ACG 370** Personal Financial Planning -- Investment, retirement, estate tax, income tax planning
- **ACG 465** Governmental and Not-For-Profit Accounting -- State/local governments, colleges, hospitals, fund accounting

### Graduate (ACG 5xx/6xx)
- **ACG 515** Preparing for MSA Success -- Case analysis, graduate expectations
- **ACG 602** Advanced Assurance and Professional Oversight -- AICPA, SEC, PCAOB governance
- **ACG 603** Corporate Governance in the 21st Century -- Sarbanes-Oxley, IFRS, XBRL, global governance
- **ACG 607** Accounting Project Design and Analysis -- Project management in accounting
- **ACG 608** Accounting Analytics -- Big data analytics with business intelligence software
- **ACG 609** Accounting Theory -- Accounting standard-setting theory, income recognition, FARS research
- **ACG 620** Analysis of Multinational Accounting -- International reporting problems, policy analysis
- **ACG 622** Research, Communication and Analysis -- Professional databases, research methods, business communication
- **ACG 623** Analysis of Management Control Systems -- Transfer pricing, budgets, compensation, behavioral considerations
- **ACG 624** Analysis of Income Tax Accounting -- Deferred tax, temporary differences, uncertain tax positions
- **ACG 625** Analysis of Governmental Accounting -- Federal/state/local government accounting procedures
