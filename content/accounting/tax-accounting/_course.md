# Tax Accounting (ACG 351 Corporate Taxation / ACG 352 Individual Taxation)

Bryant's tax courses cover federal tax structure with emphasis on individual and corporate taxation. Topics include income determination, exemptions, deductions, property transactions, accounting methods, and tax planning.

## Prerequisites
- ACG 203 and junior standing

## Key Topics
1. Tax Accounting Basics (filing status, taxable income, tax rates, credits vs deductions)
