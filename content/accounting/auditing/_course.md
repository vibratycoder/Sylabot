# Auditing Concepts and Applications (ACG 442)

Presents the basic concepts and procedures associated with an audit of financial statements. Topics include auditors' professional responsibilities, risk analysis, the nature and use of audit evidence, and audit data analytics.

## Prerequisites
- Senior standing, grade of "C" or higher in ACG 302 and ACG 345
