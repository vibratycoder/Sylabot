# English and Cultural Studies

The study of literature, writing, and cultural analysis. Bryant's English program develops critical thinking, analytical writing, and cultural literacy.

## Course Areas
- **Composition** -- Academic writing, research, argumentation
- **Literature** -- Fiction, poetry, drama, literary analysis
- **Professional Writing** -- Technical writing, editing, digital content
- **Cultural Studies** -- Film, media, popular culture, critical theory
