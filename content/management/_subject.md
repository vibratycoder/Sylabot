# Management

The art and science of planning, organizing, leading, and controlling organizations. Bryant's management program covers organizational behavior, strategic thinking, human resources, and operations.

## Course Areas
- **Principles of Management** (MGT 200) -- Management functions, organizational design
- **Organizational Behavior** (MGT 301) -- Motivation, leadership, team dynamics
- **Human Resource Management** -- Recruitment, compensation, labor relations
- **Strategic Management** -- Competitive strategy, industry analysis
- **Operations Management** -- Supply chain, quality, process improvement
- **Business Ethics** -- CSR, ethical decision-making frameworks
