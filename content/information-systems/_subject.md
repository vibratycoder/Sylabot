# Information Systems

The design, implementation, and management of technology solutions for business. Bryant's IS program covers databases, systems development, cybersecurity, and digital transformation.

## Course Areas
- **Intro to Information Systems** -- Technology foundations, business applications
- **Database Management** -- Relational model, SQL, data modeling
- **Systems Analysis and Design** -- SDLC, requirements, UML
- **Cybersecurity** -- Threats, defenses, policy, risk management
- **Networking** -- TCP/IP, infrastructure, cloud computing
- **Web Development** -- Frontend, backend, full-stack applications
