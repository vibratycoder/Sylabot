# International Business

The study of business operations across national borders. Bryant's IB program covers global strategy, cross-cultural management, international finance, and trade policy.

## Course Areas
- **Intro to International Business** -- Globalization, trade theory, market entry
- **Cross-Cultural Management** -- Cultural dimensions, negotiation, expatriate management
- **International Marketing** -- Global branding, adaptation vs standardization
- **International Finance** -- Foreign exchange, risk management, global capital markets
