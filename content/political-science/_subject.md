# Political Science

The study of government, public policy, and political behavior. Bryant's political science courses cover American government, international relations, and comparative politics.

## Course Areas
- **American Government** (PSC 150) -- Constitution, branches, federalism, civil liberties
- **International Relations** -- IR theories, conflict, diplomacy, global institutions
- **Comparative Politics** -- Political systems, democratization, development
- **Public Policy** -- Policy process, analysis, evaluation
