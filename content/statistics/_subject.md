# Statistics

The science of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data. Bryant's statistics courses support both business analytics and social science research.

## Course Areas
- **Intro to Statistics** (STAT 200) -- Descriptive stats, probability, distributions
- **Business Statistics** -- Hypothesis testing, regression, ANOVA
- **Applied Statistics** -- Multivariate analysis, nonparametric methods
