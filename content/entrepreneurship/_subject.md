# Entrepreneurship

The process of designing, launching, and managing new business ventures. Bryant's entrepreneurship program covers opportunity recognition, business planning, venture financing, and innovation.

## Course Areas
- **Intro to Entrepreneurship** -- Opportunity recognition, lean startup, business models
- **New Venture Creation** -- Business plans, market validation, pitching
- **Venture Finance** -- Bootstrapping, angel investing, venture capital
- **Innovation and Design Thinking** -- Ideation, prototyping, iteration
