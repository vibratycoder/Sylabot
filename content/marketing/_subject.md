# Marketing

The process of creating, communicating, and delivering value to customers. Bryant's marketing program covers consumer behavior, digital strategy, market research, and brand management.

## Course Areas
- **Principles of Marketing** (MKT 201) -- Marketing mix, STP, consumer behavior
- **Consumer Behavior** -- Decision-making, psychology of buying
- **Marketing Research** -- Surveys, data analysis, market intelligence
- **Digital Marketing** -- Social media, SEO, content marketing, analytics
- **Brand Management** -- Brand equity, positioning, strategy
- **Sales Management** -- Personal selling, sales force management
