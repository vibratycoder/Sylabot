# Psychology

The scientific study of behavior and mental processes. Bryant's psychology program covers developmental, social, abnormal, and applied psychology with connections to business and organizational behavior.

## Course Areas
- **Intro to Psychology** (PSY 150) -- Major perspectives, research methods, brain/behavior
- **Social Psychology** -- Attitudes, conformity, group behavior, prejudice
- **Developmental Psychology** -- Lifespan development, cognitive/social stages
- **Abnormal Psychology** -- Psychological disorders, diagnosis, treatment
- **Applied Psychology** -- I/O psychology, health psychology, forensic
- **Research Methods** -- Experimental design, ethics, statistical analysis
