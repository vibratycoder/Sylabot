# History

The study of past events and their impact on the present. Bryant's history courses cover American history, world civilizations, and thematic topics in modern history.

## Course Areas
- **US History Survey** -- Colonial era through present day
- **World History** -- Global civilizations, trade, conflict, cultural exchange
- **Modern History** -- 20th century, world wars, Cold War, globalization
